Fugus e-SIWES portal

Student training (SIWES) portal

The e-SIWES portal was developed in order to enhance the manual task of carrying out SIWES activities (carried out by students, supervisors, SIWES coordinators and the administrators during the course of the SIWES) such as sharing acceptance and placement letter to student, registration, dissemination of information, filling of log book for studentsâ€™ day-to-day activities and supervision/assessment by supervisors.


The portal is web based and allows all tasks to be carried out using the personal computer and the Internet. The digitized SIWES logbook and assessment forms for filling by students and grading by the supervisors electronically.


This will allow supervisors to be assigned immediately the students commence their industrial training and facilitate their monitoring in real-time. With the e-SIWES portal, important messages can be broadcast to students on regular basis, by their coordinator or throught news page of the portal.
